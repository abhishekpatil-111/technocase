About Us




IICMR MCA Department

IICMR stands amongst the top MCA Institutes in Pune, known for its commitment to excellence and high academic standards and reaccredited by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC), attesting to its quality and effectiveness. The MCA program boasts a substantial intake capacity, accommodating up to 120 talented students.

At the helm of the MCA department is the esteemed Dr. Deepali Sawai, a seasoned professional with a rich background of more than 25 years in both industry and academia. Under her guidance, the department is dedicated to providing a nurturing and intellectually stimulating environment for our students. Our team of dedicated professors in the MCA department collectively possesses a wealth of experience in their respective fields. Their expertise contributes significantly to the high standards of education and knowledge dissemination that we uphold.

The MCA programme at IICMR focuses on evolving areas of Information Technology and strives to cultivate skilled professionals for both the corporate and software industry sectors. The program provides robust emphasis on practical, skill-based learning. We take great pride in providing a research-oriented curriculum that redefines the conventional academic environment, fostering innovative thinking and honing problem-solving skills among our students. IICMR is committed in nurturing the next generation of leaders and experts in the field of computer applications through our forward-thinking educational approach.

The MCA curriculum encompasses a comprehensive blend of theoretical and practical aspects. It covers a wide spectrum, ranging from computer software development to mathematical computing, software technology, as well as communication and networking applications. Our state-of-the-art facilities include ICT-enabled classrooms, a dedicated GD Hall, seminar rooms, and an advanced ERP system, along with the MOODLE Learning Management System, all of which enhance the learning experience for our students.
